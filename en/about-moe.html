About Moe

Since 2010, tattooing has not only been my profession but my calling - driven by a deep demand for quality, aesthetics, and individuality.

Every tattoo is created in close exchange with the person who will wear it - as an expressive unique piece that fits the body and the story.

My artistic development was shaped by numerous projects, international guest spots, and encounters with other artists - each station on this path has allowed me to grow and further refine my signature style.

Today I work in my own private tattoo studio in Stuttgart East. There I design individual tattoos in a quiet, focused atmosphere - from graphic concepts to Black & Grey and color-intensive works. I combine technical know-how, a trained eye for composition, and a deep sense for shapes and skin.

I take my time - for the idea, the design, the implementation.

My standard: To create tattoos that not only look good today but accompany you in the long term - as part of your identity.

Take a look at my gallery - and if you feel that you are ready for a real piece: Then let's talk.
