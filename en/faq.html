Frequently Asked Questions

How old do I have to be?

You must be at least 18 years old! Even if you have parental consent, I make NO EXCEPTIONS!

The following circumstances exclude a tattoo:

  • Pregnancy / breastfeeding
  • After surgery (depending on health status)
  • While taking antibiotics (min. 4 weeks break, only then will I tattoo you)
  • Hepatitis (all forms)
  • HIV
  • Hemophilia
  • Fever
  • Neurodermatitis on the skin area to be tattooed
  • Acne on the skin area to be tattooed
  • Under the influence of alcohol, heavy drugs, or medication
  • When using numbing creams or similar
  • Cardiovascular disorders, dizziness, or risk of fainting
  • Disruptive behavior during the session
  • Unhygienic appearance at the appointment

Can I just drop by for a consultation?
Initial contact for new clients is exclusively via the contact function here on the website! Once that has taken place, I will contact you to arrange a personal appointment for a detailed consultation. I work in a studio at my private address, which is why you cannot simply drop by spontaneously/unannounced.

Where is the studio?
My studio is at Nikolaus Str. 6; 70190 Stuttgart-Ost, Stöckach stop.

How long do I have to wait for an appointment?
Usually, you get an appointment relatively quickly. The actual waiting time depends on how long I work on your design. If you plan to get tattooed only in a few months, please contact me only about 2-4 weeks before.

When will I see the design?
As soon as I feel comfortable with my design, I will send it to you via WhatsApp or email. You should familiarize yourself with my work and styles beforehand to know what you are getting into. The best tattoos are always created if you don't restrict me, so give me a free hand.

Can you send me the design / do you also tattoo drawings by others?
I only draw if we have also arranged an appointment to tattoo, so you cannot get my drawings tattooed elsewhere! My work is based on trust, so if you have my design tattooed by another tattoo artist, you harm not only their reputation, but mainly mine.

How much does a tattoo cost?
  • For an appointment I need a deposit of 50€ to 200€, depending on the size of the motif!
  • The account details will be sent to you by email with the appointment proposal.
  • If you do not appear on the agreed date or do not cancel 5 AT in advance, the deposit will be retained!

How do I pay?
Billing is by the hour. Applying the motif, preparation, and follow-up count as working time. Payment is exclusively in cash at the end of each session. How long a session lasts can almost never be known in advance, as several factors count. So please remember to bring enough cash.

How do I care for my tattoo?
If you do not have experience with tattoo care yet, you will of course receive care instructions from me via Whatsapp. For 5€ you can get a tattoo care ointment.

What do I need to consider before the appointment?
Please appear at your appointment well-groomed, sober, and healthy! If I find otherwise, I reserve the right not to start the session and retain the deposit. Despite observing the greatest possible hygiene, complications can occur. Allergic reactions and inflammation are possible risks. Tattoos are always an injury to the skin and connective tissue. It is not possible for the tattoo artist to rule out this risk completely in advance.

Spelling for text tattoos
For text tattoos, the customer is responsible for the correctness of the spelling until final approval by them! For Roman numerals, quotes in foreign languages, names, and dates of any kind, please always check yourself if everything is correct in the template. Later changes are only possible to a limited extent.

Information for allergy sufferers
All tattoo colors used are tested, approved, and certified by the Federal Health Office. Some colors contain small amounts of nickel, cadmium, or titanium, which may cause minor reactions during the healing phase, but usually disappear after a few days, except perhaps with known allergies. No test tattoos are made in this regard.
